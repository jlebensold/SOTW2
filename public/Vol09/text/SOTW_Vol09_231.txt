Volumes IV, and VI continue publication of American and European addresses.
Volumes VII and can be bound together. They contain the glorious compila-
tions on "The Divine Art of Living" and "The Covenant of God" and a wealth of dynamic,
life-giving extracts and Tablets. They form a wonderful volume for the attainment of
that supreme goal, "pure and sanctified living," which alone, as the Master says, can
spread the truth in this the dawn of the Day of God.
Volume IX contains the remarkable and exhaustive compilation of Mrs. Hannen on
"Bahai Methods of Education" and that casket of jewels, the "Stories Children Should
Know." It is also full of the latest news and Tablets from the Holy Land since the
great war has ended.
The editors of the STAR or THE will be glad to prepare these volumes for the
friends or have bound the copies sent in to them. Volume I comes at $2 each, Volumes
II, IV, V, VI, VII, IX at $2.50 apiece. If volumes VII and are bound
together they will cost $4.00 for the two. If copies are sent in they will be bound for
$1--all the bindings in three-fourths leather. Postage added to these prices.
